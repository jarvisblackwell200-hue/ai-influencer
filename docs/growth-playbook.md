# Growth Playbook

## Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-3, Target: 0 to 10K followers)

### Content Strategy
- Post consistently: 1-2x daily across platforms
- Focus on content-market fit — test what resonates
- Use trending sounds and formats on TikTok
- Cross-post everywhere to maximize reach
- No monetization yet — pure growth focus

### Growth Tactics
- **Hashtag strategy:** Mix of large (1M+), medium (100K-1M), and niche (<100K) tags
- **Engagement pods:** Interact genuinely with 20-30 accounts in the niche daily
- **Collaboration:** Duets, stitches, and response videos to larger creators
- **SEO:** Optimize YouTube descriptions, use keyword-rich captions
- **Consistency:** Same posting times daily to train the algorithm

### Key Metrics
- Follower growth rate: 10-15% weekly
- Engagement rate: 3-5% Instagram, 5-8% TikTok
- Content approval rate (first review pass)
- Time from generation to publish: < 30 min

## Phase 2: Initial Revenue (Month 3-6, Target: 10K to 50K)

### Monetization
- **Affiliate marketing:** AI tools, SaaS products, trading platforms
  - Target: 30-50% commission on digital products
  - Platforms: ShareASale, PartnerStack, individual programs
  - Swedish angle: Avanza, Nordnet affiliate programs
- **Platform monetization:** YouTube Partner Program, TikTok Creator Fund
- **Email list building:** Lead magnet (free AI tools guide / investing checklist)

### Revenue Target: $100-2,000/month

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 6-12, Target: 50K+)

### Monetization Expansion
- **Brand sponsorships:** $500-$5,000 per post
- **Digital products:** Templates, guides, mini-courses
- **Premium content:** Patreon/Fanvue for exclusive insights
- **Agency services:** Offer AI influencer creation as a service

### Revenue Target: $2,000-15,000/month

## Key Success Metrics (Track Weekly)
- Follower growth rate
- Engagement rate per platform
- Revenue per 1K followers
- Cost per post (API costs / posts published)
- Content approval rate
- Time from generation to publish
