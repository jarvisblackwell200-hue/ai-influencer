# Content Calendar

## Monthly Theme Rotation

### Week 1: Educational
- How-to guides and explainers
- "Did you know?" facts about AI/tech
- Tool reviews and comparisons
- Step-by-step tutorials

### Week 2: Trend Commentary
- React to AI/tech news
- Hot takes on industry developments
- "What this means for you" breakdowns
- Predictions and analysis

### Week 3: Personal / Lifestyle
- Day-in-the-life content
- Behind-the-scenes of the tech world
- Stockholm life and Nordic culture
- "What I'm working on" updates

### Week 4: Engagement
- Polls and questions ("Which AI tool do you prefer?")
- Challenges and interactive content
- Community spotlights
- Q&A sessions
