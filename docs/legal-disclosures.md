# Legal Disclosures & Compliance

## Bio Disclosure (All Platforms)

Include in every platform bio:
```
AI-generated content | Virtual Creator
```

Or variations:
- "Virtual AI creator sharing tech insights"
- "AI-powered content creator | All content AI-generated"
- "Digital creator powered by AI"

## Sponsored Post Disclosure

### Required by FTC (USA) and EU regulations:
- Place `#ad` or `#sponsored` at the **beginning** of the caption (not buried in hashtags)
- Must be visible without clicking "more"
- If video: verbal disclosure in first 3 seconds + text overlay

### Format:
```
#ad | Partnering with [Brand] to bring you this...
[Rest of caption]
```

### Swedish Law (Marknadsforingslagen):
- Swedish consumers must be clearly informed of advertising
- Use "Annons" or "Reklam" for Swedish-language content
- The commercial nature must be obvious and not misleading

## EU AI Act Compliance (Effective 2025)

### Requirements for AI-generated content:
1. **Transparency obligation**: Users must be informed that content is AI-generated
2. **Deep fake disclosure**: AI-generated images/videos of a person (even fictional) must be labeled
3. **Watermarking**: Best practice to include AI generation metadata in images

### Implementation:
- Bio disclosure on all platforms
- Add text overlay "AI Generated" on video content (small, lower corner)
- Include in image metadata: "Generated by AI (Flux 2 Max)"
- Caption disclosure: Include "AI-generated" in first comment or caption where applicable

## Affiliate Disclosure

### Required format:
```
This post contains affiliate links. I may earn a commission if you make a purchase through these links, at no extra cost to you.
```

### Short version for captions:
```
#affiliate | Links in bio may earn me a commission
```
